Score Calculation

Business Requirements Implementation

Volunteers who proved their expertise, will go through your assignments and rate it. You will have to meet all the assignment requirements in order to get more score. Also the code should be self explainatory and well documented. If someone can't understand your document or code, that's considered as no assignment done!

Note: BRI (Business Requirements Implementation) score will have 1.0 weight on final score.

Learning Analytics (LA)

Our Machine Learning algorithm will generate a score based on learning analytics during the time work on the assignment. It's not about the quantity but quality. Our ML algorithm is learning every day from thousand of entries!

If you don't have Learning Analytics (LA) installed in your Chrome browser, please use this link to install it. bit.ly/learning_analytics_101

Note: LA score will have 0.7 weight on final score.

Problem Solving Archive

You can collect the problems you faced during your assignment and share with us in the simple text format.
Note: PSA (Problem Solving Archive) score will have 0.8 weight on final score.
